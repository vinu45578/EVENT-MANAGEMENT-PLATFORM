
## Stakeholder Identification

Stakeholders are individuals or groups who have an interest in or are affected by the **Event Management Platform**. Identifying stakeholders helps in understanding their needs, expectations, and influence on the system.

### Identified Stakeholders

#### 1. End Users
- **Students**  
  Students use the platform to view events, register for participation, and enroll as volunteers.
- **Student Volunteers**  
  Volunteers use the system to sign up for events, receive updates, and track their participation history.

These users rely on the platform for timely information, easy registration, and transparent participation records.

#### 2. Event Organizers / Administrators
- **College Administration**
- **Faculty Coordinators**
- **Event Organizers**

They are responsible for creating, managing, and monitoring events, handling volunteer enrollment, tracking attendance, and ensuring smooth execution of events.

#### 3. Project Team
- **Project Manager**
- **Developers**
- **QA Lead**
- **Documentation Lead**

The project team is responsible for planning, designing, developing, testing, and documenting the Event Management Platform.

#### 4. Faculty / Evaluator
- **SEAD Course Instructor**

The faculty evaluates the project based on problem understanding, system design, implementation quality, and documentation.

#### 5. System Administrator
- Responsible for maintaining the platform, managing user access, handling data storage, and ensuring system reliability and security.

Identifying these stakeholders ensures that the Event Management Platform is designed to meet the needs and expectations of all involved parties and supports efficient event coordination within the institution.
