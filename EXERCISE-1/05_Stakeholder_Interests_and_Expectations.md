## Stakeholder Interests and Expectations

This document outlines the interests and expectations of the stakeholders involved in the **Event Management Platform**.

### 1. End Users

**Interests:**
- Easy access to information about upcoming events
- Simple registration and volunteering process
- Timely notifications and updates

**Expectations:**
- Accurate event details and schedules
- Seamless registration and attendance tracking
- Clear records of participation for certificates and recognition

### 2. Event Organizers / Administrators

**Interests:**
- Efficient event creation and management
- Better coordination with participants and volunteers
- Reduced manual work and errors

**Expectations:**
- Centralized control over events and registrations
- Reliable attendance and volunteer tracking
- Automated communication and notifications

### 3. Project Team

**Interests:**
- Successful completion of the project
- Proper implementation of functional requirements
- Gaining practical experience in software development

**Expectations:**
- Clearly defined requirements
- Effective teamwork and communication
- Timely completion of assigned tasks

### 4. Faculty / Evaluator

**Interests:**
- Proper application of SEAD principles
- Clear system architecture and design
- Well-organized documentation

**Expectations:**
- Complete and structured project submission
- Logical design with justification
- Adherence to academic and evaluation guidelines

### 5. System Administrator

**Interests:**
- Smooth and reliable system operation
- Secure data handling and maintenance

**Expectations:**
- Stable system performance
- Proper data management and backups
- Minimal system downtime and errors
