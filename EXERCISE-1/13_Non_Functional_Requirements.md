## Non-Functional Requirements

Non-functional requirements describe the quality attributes and constraints of the **Event Management Platform**.

### NFR1: Usability
The system shall provide a simple and user-friendly interface that can be easily used by students, faculty, and event organizers with minimal training.

### NFR2: Performance
The system shall respond to user actions such as event loading, registration, and attendance updates within an acceptable time under normal operating conditions.

### NFR3: Reliability
The system shall function correctly during normal usage and ensure consistency of event, registration, and attendance data without loss.

### NFR4: Security
The system shall ensure that user data is protected through authentication and role-based access control, allowing access only to authorized users.

### NFR5: Maintainability
The system shall be modular and well-documented to allow easy maintenance and future enhancements.

### NFR6: Compatibility
The system shall be compatible with commonly used web browsers such as Chrome, Edge, and Firefox.

These non-functional requirements ensure that the Event Management Platform delivers a reliable, secure, and efficient user experience.
