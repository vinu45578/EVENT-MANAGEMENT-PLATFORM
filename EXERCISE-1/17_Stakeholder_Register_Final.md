## Stakeholder Register (Final)

This document presents the finalized stakeholder register for the **Event Management Platform** after reviewing stakeholder roles, responsibilities, and levels of influence.

| Stakeholder | Role | Responsibility | Influence |
|------------|------|----------------|-----------|
| Students | End User | View events, register, and participate | Medium |
| Student Volunteers | End User | Enroll as volunteers and assist in events | Medium |
| Event Organizers | Administrator | Create, manage, and coordinate events | High |
| College Administration | Administrator | Monitor events and student participation | High |
| Project Manager | Project Team | Project planning, coordination, and monitoring | High |
| Lead Developer | Project Team | System design and development | High |
| QA Lead | Project Team | Testing and quality assurance | Medium |
| Documentation Lead | Project Team | Preparation and maintenance of documentation | Medium |
| Faculty / Evaluator | Academic Reviewer | Project assessment and evaluation | High |
| System Administrator | Technical Support | System maintenance and data management | Medium |

This final stakeholder register ensures that all stakeholders involved in the **Event Management Platform** are clearly identified along with their responsibilities and level of influence, supporting effective project planning and execution.
