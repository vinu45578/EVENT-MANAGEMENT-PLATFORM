
## Team Agreement

### Introduction
This document defines the team agreement for the development of the **Event Management Platform**. The agreement is intended to ensure smooth collaboration, clear responsibility sharing, and effective communication among all team members throughout the project lifecycle.

### Team Roles and Responsibilities

- **Project Manager**  
  Oversees overall project planning, coordination, progress tracking, and decision-making.

- **Documentation Lead**  
  Responsible for preparing, updating, and maintaining all project-related documentation.

- **Lead Developer**  
  Handles system architecture, design, development, and implementation of core features.

- **QA Lead**  
  Ensures system quality through testing, validation, and verification of requirements.

### Communication Plan
- Team communication will be conducted through **WhatsApp** and **in-person discussions**.
- Regular meetings will be held to review progress, discuss challenges, and plan upcoming tasks.

### Decision Making
- Decisions will be made through **mutual discussion and consensus** among team members.
- In case of conflicts or unresolved issues, the **Project Manager** will take the final decision.

### Work Ethics
- All team members commit to completing assigned tasks within agreed timelines.
- Team members will support and assist each other whenever required.
- Any issues or delays will be communicated early to minimize risks and ensure smooth progress.

### Agreement Acceptance
All team members agree to follow and adhere to this team agreement throughout the **Event Management Platform** project lifecycle.
