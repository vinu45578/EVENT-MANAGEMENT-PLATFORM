## Activity Identification

Activities represent the flow of actions performed in the **Event Management Platform**.

### Identified Activities

- User login
- Viewing available events
- Registering for events
- Enrolling as a volunteer
- Creating events (administrator)
- Managing event details
- Tracking attendance
- Viewing participation and volunteering history

These activities define the functional flow of the **Event Management Platform** and help in modeling system behavior during the design phase.
